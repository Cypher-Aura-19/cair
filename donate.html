FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! CAIR Michigan is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You'll receive a tax receipt via email.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Absolutely. You can modify or cancel your recurring donation at any time by contacting us.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your card details.

Is Zakat applicable?

Yes, certain CAIR Michigan programs are Zakat-eligible. Contact us for specific guidance.

Can I donate in someone's honor?

Yes, you can make a tribute gift in honor or memory of someone during checkout.

Does my employer match donations?

Many employers match charitable gifts. Check with your HR department to double your impact.
