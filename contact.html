FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I report a civil rights violation?

You can report through our online form, call our hotline, or visit our office. All reports are kept strictly confidential.

Do you offer free legal services?

Yes, we provide pro-bono legal assistance for civil rights cases including discrimination, harassment, and hate crimes.

Can I request a speaker for my event?

Absolutely! Visit our Request a Speaker page to submit your event details and topic preferences.

How can I volunteer with CAIR?

We welcome volunteers! Contact us through the form above or email us directly to learn about current opportunities.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, CAIR Michigan is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit. All donations are fully tax-deductible.

What are your response times?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Urgent matters are prioritized immediately.
