Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and advocacy.

How do I report a civil rights violation?

You can use the form above by selecting "Report an Incident" or call our hotline directly. All reports are confidential.

Do you offer free legal representation?

Yes, for cases that fall within our scope (civil rights, discrimination), we provide pro-bono legal services.

How can I have someone speak at my event?

Select "General Inquiry" in the contact form and provide details about your event, date, and topic.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, CAIR Michigan is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible.
