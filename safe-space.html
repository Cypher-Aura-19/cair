Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To proactively cultivate inclusive environments in the public and private sector for religious practice and expression through advocacy, education, dialogue, and partnership.

Our Vision

To normalize and protect Islamic practice and expression within American society and policy without stripping Muslims of their core values and beliefs.

Safe Spaces has successfully represented clients of discrimination, drafted & implemented legal policies, hosted training & development sessions, and fostered productive partnerships with a range of institutions including schools/universities, correctional facilities, law enforcement, government agencies, as well as corporations throughout Michigan.

Our Core Beliefs

How We Serve the Community

Our work is guided by four core principles

01

Engaging

Promoting civic and political engagement concerning state/national policies that curtail or support the rights of American Muslims.

02

Mediating

Preserving and protecting the constitutional rights of clients in response to anti-Muslim bigotry.

03

Accommodating

Upholding the right to religious practice and expression by ensuring proper arrangements and providing the necessary tools for integration.

04

Educating

Providing accurate information about Muslims and Islam with guidance on how to effectively accommodate Muslims.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive support for institutions and individuals across Michigan

01

School Bullying & Censorship

Safe Spaces works proactively with schools to implement religious-friendly policies and provide guidance in the forms of diversity and cultural sensitivity training to better combat racism and anti-Muslim bigotry.

File a Complaint
02

Employment Discrimination

Safe Spaces provides representation to those who have experienced discrimination in the workplace and educational institutions.

File a Complaint
03

Halal Food Accommodations

Safe Spaces works with schools and universities to implement halal food in school cafeterias. We also work with police departments, government agencies, and other institutions to offer halal food options.

Contact Us
04

Religious Accommodations for Prisoners

Safe Spaces advocates on behalf of Muslims housed at correctional facilities seeking religious accommodations such as access to hijabs and Qurans, timely Ramadan meals, and much more.

Learn More
05

Diversity & Sensitivity Training

Safe Spaces designed its diversity training to help corporations, private institutions, and government agencies reflect cultural competency in their attitudes, policies, and services.

Request Training
06

National Advocacy Week

Safe Spaces partners with the U.S. Council of Muslim Organizations (USCMO) each year to lead Muslim constituents across the nation in meetings with Congressmen and women.
