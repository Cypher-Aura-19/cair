Quick Tips

Remember These Key Points

01

Stay Calm

Remain calm and polite. Don't argue, resist, or obstruct officers even if you believe your rights are being violated.

02

Ask Questions

Ask "Am I free to leave?" If yes, calmly walk away. If no, you are being detained and have the right to remain silent.

03

Request a Lawyer

Say "I want to speak to a lawyer" and then remain silent. Do not answer questions without legal representation.

04

Document Everything

Write down badge numbers, patrol car numbers, and agency. Get witness contact information and take photos if safe.

Need Legal Help?
