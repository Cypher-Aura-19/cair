Our Mission

Why Volunteer With CAIR-MI?

CAIR-MI's mission is to enhance the understanding of Islam, encourage dialogue, protect civil liberties, empower American Muslims and build coalitions which promote justice and mutual understanding.

By volunteering, you become part of a movement dedicated to civil rights advocacy and community empowerment.
