General Information

  • Interns work a minimum of 10-15 hours per week during a 12-week internship period
  • Internships are offered during all semesters
  • Interns may receive academic credit
  • All internships are unpaid
  • Legal internships are subject to specific criteria

Eligibility

  • Internships are open to undergraduate and graduate students
  • Legal internships are available to year 2 law students
  • High school students may apply for Special Project Internships

Application Procedure

  • Submit a cover letter, resume, and references
  • Legal internship applicants should include a legal writing sample
  • Selected candidates will be contacted for an interview
