We Fight For You

Cases We Handle for American Muslims

Our attorneys specialize in discrimination and civil rights violations targeting Muslims

Workplace Discrimination

Fired, not hired, or harassed because you're Muslim? We can help.

  • Denied Jummah prayer time
  • Hijab or beard restrictions
  • Ramadan accommodation denied

School & Student Issues

Muslim children deserve safe schools free from bullying and discrimination.

  • Anti-Muslim bullying
  • Hijab dress code issues
  • Prayer room denied

FBI & Police Encounters

Questioned, surveilled, or profiled by law enforcement? Know your rights.

  • FBI home visits
  • Mosque surveillance
  • Profiling & harassment

Airport & Travel Profiling

Detained, searched, or questioned every time you fly? We fight back.

  • No-fly list issues
  • Secondary screening
  • Phone/laptop searches

Hate Crimes & Islamophobia

Attacked, threatened, or harassed for being Muslim? Report it to us.

  • Physical attacks
  • Hijab pulled off
  • Mosque vandalism

Public & Housing Discrimination

Refused service or housing because of your faith or appearance?

  • Denied apartment/home
  • Refused service
  • Mosque zoning blocked

Cases Outside Our Scope

We do not handle: General criminal defense, divorce/family law, immigration applications, personal injury, or contract disputes. For these matters, contact the State Bar of Michigan.
