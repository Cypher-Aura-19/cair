Our Expertise

Types of Cases We Handle

Our legal team specializes in civil rights violations affecting the Muslim community

Employment Discrimination

Wrongful termination, hiring bias, religious accommodation denials, workplace harassment

  • Denied prayer breaks
  • Hijab/beard restrictions
  • Hostile work environment

Education & Schools

Student bullying, administrative bias, religious expression restrictions

  • Bullying & harassment
  • Dress code violations
  • Curriculum bias

Law Enforcement

Police misconduct, FBI interrogations, surveillance, profiling

  • Unlawful questioning
  • Profiling incidents
  • Excessive force

Travel & Border

Airport profiling, no-fly list issues, CBP encounters, visa problems

  • Secondary screening
  • Device searches
  • Entry denials

Hate Crimes & Speech

Physical attacks, verbal harassment, vandalism, online threats

  • Physical assaults
  • Property damage
  • Threatening messages

Public Accommodations

Denial of services, restaurant/store discrimination, housing bias

  • Service refusals
  • Housing discrimination
  • Public facility access

Cases Outside Our Scope

We do not handle: General criminal defense, divorce/family law, immigration applications, personal injury, or contract disputes. For these matters, contact the State Bar of Michigan.
