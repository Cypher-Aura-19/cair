Speaking Topics

What We Can Speak About

Our speakers are equipped to address a wide range of topics tailored to your audience's needs.

Know Your Rights (FBI & Police)

What to do if the FBI knocks on your door, your rights during police encounters, and how to protect yourself legally.

Workplace Rights for Muslims

Jummah prayer accommodations, hijab rights, Ramadan scheduling, and fighting workplace discrimination.

Islam 101 for Non-Muslims

Dispelling myths, explaining Islamic practices, and building understanding between Muslims and neighbors.

Muslim Youth Identity & Leadership

Empowering young Muslims to be confident in their faith, engage civically, and become community leaders.

Airport & Travel Rights

What to do during secondary screening, device searches, and CBP encounters at the border.

Combating Islamophobia

Understanding the Islamophobia industry, responding to hate, and effective advocacy strategies.
