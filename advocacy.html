Our Focus Areas

What We Advocate For

Our advocacy efforts span multiple critical areas affecting civil rights

Challenging Anti-Muslim Legislation

We track and oppose bills targeting Muslims—from "anti-Sharia" laws to discriminatory surveillance programs.

  • Opposing anti-Muslim bills
  • Testifying at hearings
  • Building legislative allies

Religious Freedom Protection

Defending the right to wear hijab, grow beards, pray at work, and practice Islam freely without discrimination.

  • Hijab & religious dress rights
  • Prayer accommodation laws
  • Halal food access

Travel Ban & Profiling

Fighting Muslim travel bans, no-fly list abuses, and discriminatory airport profiling that targets our community.

  • No-fly list challenges
  • CBP accountability
  • Visa discrimination

Ending Muslim Surveillance

Challenging CVE programs, mosque surveillance, and unconstitutional spying on Muslim communities.

  • Ending CVE programs
  • Mosque surveillance reform
  • FBI accountability

Law Enforcement Training

Working with police departments to end profiling and provide cultural competency training about Islam and Muslims.

  • Anti-profiling policies
  • Cultural awareness training
  • Community-police dialogue

Combating Islamophobia

Challenging anti-Muslim hate speech, media bias, and the Islamophobia industry that spreads fear about our community.

  • Hate crime legislation
  • Media accountability
  • School anti-bullying
Our Approach

How We Create Change

Our multi-pronged approach combines grassroots organizing, coalition building, and strategic engagement to achieve lasting policy change.

01

Research & Analysis

We thoroughly research issues, analyze proposed legislation, and develop evidence-based policy positions.

02

Coalition Building

We partner with diverse organizations to amplify our voice and build broad-based support for civil rights.

03

Direct Engagement

We meet with legislators, testify at hearings, and engage directly with decision-makers.

04

Community Mobilization

We empower community members to participate in advocacy through action alerts and campaigns.
